Description

This analysis projects the price of BTC from 2025 to 2040 under different growth and decline scenarios. It assumes:

  • Bear Case: 8% annual growth with 15% decline every fourth year.
  • Base Case: 15% annual growth with 25% decline every fourth year.
  • Bull Case: 22% annual growth with 30% decline every fourth year.
  • Uber Bull Case: 45% annual growth with 40% decline every fourth year.

Initial multipliers of 1.3, 1.45, 1.6, and 1.8 are applied for Bear, Base, Bull, and Uber Bull cases, respectively.
