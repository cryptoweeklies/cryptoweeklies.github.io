Methodology Overview

Each cryptocurrency’s upside potential is estimated using three independent risk metrics: Polynomial Regression, TWAP divergence, and SMA deviation. These are combined into composite risks to derive potential price targets under three market sentiment scenarios:

  • Base Peak: 0.6 × 0.95 × Price / CompositeRiskadjusted
  • Bull Peak: 0.95 × Price / CompositeRiskconservative
  • Moon Boy Peak: 1.5 × 0.95 × Price / CompositeRiskadjusted

Rewards are computed as Peak ÷ Price. “Adjusted” risk weighting kicks in when Regression Risk < 0.2, increasing TWAP weight by 40% and reducing Regression weight by 20% before renormalization.

This report is for research purposes only — not financial advice.
