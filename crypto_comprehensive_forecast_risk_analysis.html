The table above shows the risk analysis for various cryptocurrencies based on three calculated metrics: Regression Risk, TWAP Risk, and SMA Risk, along with their latest price.

The Weighted Risk is a weighted average of the three risk metrics, and the Bull Peak is calculated as (0.95 * Latest Price) / Weighted Risk, providing an adjusted metric based on the risks.

The Reward is the ratio of the Bull Peak to the Latest Price, providing a comparison of potential upside reward.
