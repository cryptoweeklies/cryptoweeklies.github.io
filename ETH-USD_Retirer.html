Description

This analysis projects the price of ETH from 2026 to 2041 assuming 2026 is a Bear Market year. It assumes:

  • Bear Case: 15% annual growth with 25% decline every Bear Market year (starting 2026).
  • Base Case: 25% annual growth with 30% decline every Bear Market year (starting 2026).
  • Bull Case: 45% annual growth with 50% decline every Bear Market year (starting 2026).
  • Uber Bull Case: 55% annual growth with 60% decline every Bear Market year (starting 2026).

Initial multipliers of 1.3, 1.45, 1.6, and 1.8 are applied to the current price before the 2026 decline calculation.
