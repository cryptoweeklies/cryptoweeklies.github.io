This exercise aims to generate three separate charts showing the projected BNB (BNB) prices and the amount of BNB needed to accumulate $1 million, under three different scenarios. The scenarios represent different Compound Annual Growth Rates (CAGR) for BNB prices.

The first chart represents a bearish scenario with an 8% CAGR, indicating a relatively conservative growth rate for BNB prices. The second chart depicts a base scenario with a 15% CAGR, representing a moderate growth rate. Finally, the third chart illustrates a bullish scenario with a 22% CAGR, indicating a more optimistic outlook for BNB price appreciation.

Each chart provides insights into how BNB prices may evolve over time and how many BNB units would be needed to accumulate $1 million under each scenario. These insights can help investors and enthusiasts understand potential future returns and make their own informed decisions about BNB investments. The charts are presented for easy comparison and analysis.
